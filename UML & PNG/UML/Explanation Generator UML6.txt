@startuml
title EXPLANATION GENERATOR

start

:Input Final Compatibility Score;

if (Score > 80%) then (High Fit)
    :Generate Strong Recommendation;
else (Moderate)
    :Generate Conditional Recommendation;
endif

:Generate Why Club Needs Player;

note right
Reason Derived From:
- Tactical Gap
- Statistical Underperformance
- Physical Deficiency
end note

:Generate Why Player Fits;

note right
Explain using:
- Cosine Similarity %
- Pressing Match %
- Build-up Fit %
end note

:Generate Contender Impact;

note right
Explain:
- Projected xG Gain
- Projected Goals
- Projected Points
end note

stop
@enduml
